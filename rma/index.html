    • Welcome to RMA Killeen! We are one of nine schools in the State of Texas that are part of RMA Texas Public Schools. We strive daily to ENSURE that all of our students are successful on our campus. We serve students on a four-hour school day with smaller classes than in traditional schools. Our goal is for this to help the students feel more at home. I am glad that you found our website. Our campus is ready to serve your child in the best way possible. Please contact the school if you have any questions! Thank you again for your interest in RMA-Killeen and we hope you come to visit us soon!

      Why choose Richard Milburn Academy (RMA)?

      • We offer dual credit/ college courses.
      • We offer an Accelerated Learning Program.
      • We offer small class sizes.
      • We offer credit recovery and dropout prevention interventions.
      • We are located in downtown Killeen, easily accessible for all students across the city. 
      • RMA - Killeen is one of RMA Texas Public Schools' original campuses.

      Principal's Message

      Mr. Derrick Smith, Principal

      Welcome to Richard Milburn Academy Killeen, home of the tigers. It is an honor and a privilege to serve as the Principal of RMA Killeen. We look forward to cultivating excellence in our students as well as ensuring that they are prepared to be successful post-graduation. Administrators, instructors, and district staff are working diligently to meet the needs of every student.

      This school year will be filled with excitement. There will be a schoolwide focus on improving student attendance and participation. While our goal is to ALWAYS provide a safe, rigorous, and productive learning environment, we would like to do so in a more collaborative manner. The school motto this year is “teamwork makes the dream work.” Our team is looking forward to working with students, parents, and other community members to accommodate student needs and ensure academic growth.

      I would like to remind all constituents that we have an open-door policy, so please be sure to stop by if you have any questions or concerns.
